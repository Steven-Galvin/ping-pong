Wanna Play Ping Pong?

This is a simple game, but it's not like the traditional form of ping pong. Unlike the one you may know, this one is a numbers game. With this simple web app, you can give me any postive integer and I will return you every number between one and the number you chose. There are a few rules to this game though.

The three rules are:

  • All numbers divisible by three will be replaced with the word "Ping!"
  • All numbers divisible by five will be replaced with the word "Pong!"
  • All numbers divisible by 15 will be replaced with the word "Ping-pong!"

And that's that! If you are ready to play, just pick a number, any number, and prepare to be amazed!
